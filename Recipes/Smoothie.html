Strawberry Banana Protein Shake

Description

High Protein Shake that is packed with omega 3 fatty acids.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 teaspoon flax seeds
  • .5 cup soy milk
  • .5 cup orange juice
  • 4 strawberries
  • 1 scoop protein powder
  • 1 teaspoon flax seed oil

Directions

  1. Place flax seeds in a food processor or coffee grinder; pulse until ground.
  2. Combine soy milk, orange juice, banana, strawberries, whey protein powder, ground flax seeds, and flax seed oil in a blender; blend until smooth.
